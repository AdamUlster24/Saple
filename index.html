    How to Play Saple

    • You have 6 guesses
    • Green in any column indicates a match!
    • If a pet or food is in multiple packs, the pet is considered to be in the one that was released earlier.
    • Yellow in Tier column indicates 2 tiers within the pet's/food's tier.
    • Buffers provide consistent scaling (ex. monkey).
    • Scalers benefit from stats and are generally used with buffers (ex. dog).
    • Tempos are strong without extra setup and can be buffed, as well as buff other pets short-term (ex. horse).
    • Summoners summon other pets (ex. deer).
    • Disruptors/Snipers are strong late game and scale relative to the opponents' units (ex. scorpion).
    • Economists are used to save gold/rolls (ex. pig).
    • Foods are bought, sold, or fainted to buff other pets (ex. cow and apple).
    • Specials have unique abilities and do not fall into other categories (ex. parrot).
    • Yellow in Base Attack column indicates at most a 2 difference of damage.
    • Yellow in Base Health column indicates at most a 2 difference of health.

    Ways to incorporate Saple into your Super Auto Pets runs:

    1. Whatever pets/food you guess, you must use in your next Super Auto Pets run and whatever the final pet/food is, you must get it to level 3 if possible.
    2. After every battle, you must choose one pet/food in your shop to guess
